    Research Assistance #

    Condition Value
    Definition Name of an agent for research in support of a resource.
    Required? No
    Repeatable? Yes

    Guidelines #

    For example, this field can be used to hold the names of College students and faculty
    responsible for metadata creation, translations, research, scanning, color correction, etc. It
    could also list names of experts who have provided content, criticisms, and other forms of
    assistance for a particular image.
