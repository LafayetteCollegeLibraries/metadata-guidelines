    Location

    Definition

    Spatial characteristics and/or cover of the resource content. Spatial topic and spatial applicability may be a named place or a location specified by its geographic coordinates.

    Required?

    No

    Repeatable?

    Yes


    Guidelines #

    • Spatial coverage is about the content of item and refers to a geographic location, and not to proper place names such as buildings or human-created landmarks.

    • In the repository Work dashboard, this property is configured to fetch controlled geographic location data from the GeoNames webservice .
      → Enter the geographic location into the form, then select from the list retrieved from GeoNames. This populates the value with a URI for the location, which serves as linked geographic data.

    • As an alternative to GeoNames, the LDR also supports Getty Thesaurus of Geographic Names linked data service .
      → TGN is recommended as a controlled vocabulary source if the item or collection deals primarily with historical location information, for example political or geographic names that are no longer in existence.

    • Proper names for buildings, neighborhoods, geographic regions, or other non- controlled location information can be entered in the Keyword property.
